Frequently Asked Questions

How do you vet your tutors? expand_more
Our vetting process is rigorous. Only top 1% graduates from Ivy League and equivalent institutions are considered. Each tutor undergoes multiple rounds of interviews, background checks, and a probationary teaching period.
Can I switch tutors if it's not a good fit? expand_more
Absolutely. We believe the mentor-student relationship is key to success. If the chemistry isn't right, we will rematch your child immediately at no extra cost, ensuring the perfect educational partner.
Is there a long-term contract? expand_more
No. Our monthly plans are flexible and can be cancelled or paused at any time. We believe our results will keep you with us, not a contract. Annual plans offer savings but are also refundable on a pro-rated basis within the first 30 days.
